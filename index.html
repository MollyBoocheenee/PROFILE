About me

I'm Molly. Art has always been a cornerstone of my existence. My first love was illustration, but I have experimented with photography, creative writing, and other visual art mediums. I always thought I would go into animation or concept illustration. It was only when I joined my high school yearbook that I learned that I loved print design. I soon realized, as I was building my portfolio, that graphic design was in equal parts familiar and challenging. I love being able to test what I can do in this program.
